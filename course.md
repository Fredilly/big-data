<div class="card">
  <h2>Course Philosophy</h2>
  <p>This course bridges the gap between <strong>academic programming foundations</strong> and <strong>real-world data science practice</strong>. Students will learn not just <em>how</em> to code, but <em>why</em> and <em>when</em> to apply specific data science techniques — with a strong emphasis on:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Writing production-quality data pipelines</li>
    <li>Understanding the full lifecycle of a data science project</li>
    <li>Building intuition for model selection and evaluation</li>
    <li>Communicating results effectively</li>
  </ul>
</div>
